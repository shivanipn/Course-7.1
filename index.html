About Logic Gates

Logic gates are the foundations of digital technology. They are like switches that make decisions based on one or more inputs and output a single answer. They are usually made up of diodes or transistors that work together to help computers ‚Äòthink‚Äô in a binary way.

Use of Logic Gates

Logic gates are the basic building blocks of digital integrated circuits. Logic gates are the devices that perform logical operations on one or more binary inputs and produce a single binary output. They are primarily constructed using diodes or transistors when used as a switch.
